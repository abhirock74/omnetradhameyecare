About Us

Among its various projects and activities, through the hospital and outreach programme, the Quality Eye Health Care Services are provided to the underserved population of Bihar and adjacent states.

These services are provided through well trained outreach team, Hospital’s highly qualified Outpatient department staff. Organizing free eye screening camps and establishment of Vision Centresin different locations in Bihar for the sustainable delivery of Quality Eye Care are carried out through its main initiative Mission: ‘Vision for All’.

A Part of Our Mission

To prevent blindness, restore eyesight promote eye health and aims to provide low-cost affordable access to high quality eye care services for all. To provide outdoor, indoor and speciality services viz. routine eye check-up, cataract, Glaucoma and other specialized surgeries for poor and needy patients free of cost throughout the year.

Enhance comprehensive eye care delivery oriented outreach programme, particularly in cataract surgery in the remote and underserved areas in Bihar and Jharkhand.

Develop vision centres to provide high quality primary eye care services at the doorstep of the suffering community at affordable cost
